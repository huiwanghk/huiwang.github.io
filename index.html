About

In the past 7 years, my research direction has changed from railway transportation planning and management to urban bus operation and optimization, and then to a new theme: transportation combined with geography. These are the changes in research perspective from traditional engineering to humanities and social sciences, which have also become a new challenge for me 😵‍💫.

Currently, I am a second-year PhD student at the University of Hong Kong. I have always had strong research interests in emerging issues in transportation and geography and enjoy solving these problems by adopting the STATE-OF-THE-ART methods, which will make sweeping changes to next-generation mobility 😮‍💨.

Here are my research interests🤗:

  • Research topics🙂: Planning, Management, and Optimization on Sustainable Transport
  • Research methods😀: Discrete Choice Analysis | GIS | Spatial-temporal data Analysis | Agent-based simulation
